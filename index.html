About Liz

Liz Breslin is a Front End Developer at Quicket in Cape Town, South Africa. Coming from a background in customer service and then UX design - and being a frequent customer of things herself - she's become quite familiar with multiple aspects and stages of the product life cycle. She enjoys finding clarity in complex systems and ensuring that clarity extends to the users.
